How to Use This Dashboard

Track Your Progress

The Opportunities page displays all internship and job listings with real-time status updates. Each entry shows the company name, position title, application deadline, and current status.

Manage Applications

Use the Apply form to submit your inquiry for any position. Include your preferred role, experience level, and any specific questions about the opportunity.

Plan Your Career

Organize opportunities by application deadline and role type. Focus on positions that match your skills and interests for better outcomes.

Stay Connected

Contact us for networking advice, cover letter tips, or referrals. Our resources are designed to support your journey from student to professional.

Pro Tips for Success

  1. Start Early: Begin applying 3-6 months before your desired start date.
  2. Customize Your Applications: Tailor your resume and cover letter for each role.
  3. Follow Up: Send polite follow-up emails after two weeks if you haven't heard back.
  4. Network: Attend career fairs and connect with professionals on LinkedIn.
  5. Practice Interview Skills: Use online resources to prepare for technical and behavioral interviews.
  6. Track Everything: Keep detailed notes on each application and company.
